- ABOUT -

I'm Apihaiuezuritanaka Hemi, but you can call me Api for short (I know, longest name in the world right?). A bit about me. I'm a 19 year old who is half Japanese and half Maori. And yes, I am fluent in both. My three biggest passions in life are aything to do with creativity, travel and helping others. Since from a young age, I was very fortunate enough to travel alot. Almost moving to a new town or country every year. Not only did my family have a deep love for travel and culture but, we also loved music and art. So being immersed in this lifestyle from a young age has definitely built the foundations of who I am today. I made this blog to showcase and document all the adventures to come.
