About

I am half Japanese and half New Zealander. My three biggest passions in life are travel, art and helping others. These three things are what inspired me to finally decide to learn code. From a young age, I have always been creative. From learning guitar from the age of 5, to picking up a camera and indulging myself in photography and videography.

I have also always had a interest in computers and how they worked. I was always the go to guy when it came to helping my parents with anything tech related (most of the time it was just turning it off and on). After doing a bit of research, I found that coding was one of the careers that can allow me to do what I love every day. My three passions: Travel, art and helping others.

  1. Travel: Because we are living in a digital world, the possiblities of working remotely have never been so possible. As long as you have a internet connection, you can do your job.

  3. Helping others: With this new skillset, you have the ability to cure peoples headaches and help others make their imaginations come to life.
